Boring (by dramforever)

http://dram.cf/boring/

Arrow keys to move. Don't let the ball fall below your pad. Hit the circles.

Combos are counted between to failures. A combo of n gives n2 points.

Failures lose 3 points. But if you fail with combo = 0, you lose 5 points

Shift to accelerate. Erases combo count.

When the ball has flown for 7 seconds without hitting a circle or the pad, pressing Space will make the ball fall straight down. Keeps combos.

P or Esc to pause. Press any other key to continue

The angle in which the ball bounces off the pad depends only on the point of contact
